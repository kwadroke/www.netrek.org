Team Rankings

At the end of the regular season, a team's ranking
will be determined by

	1. percentage of games won
	2. average continuous score

A tie counts as half a win.

In case of a forfeit: the forfeit counts as a loss.
For a team that "wins" a forfeited game, the game
does not count.

Playoff

At the end of the season, the teams will be ranked.
Then we'll have a playoff:

	Game A:  #1 vs #2
	Game B:  #3 vs #4
	Game C:  #5 vs #6

Then re-rank the teams based on who the winners are.
The final rankings will be:

	Champion: winner of Game A
	2nd place: loser of A
	3rd place: winner of B
	4th place: loser of B
	5th place: winner of C
	6th place: loser of C

The higher ranked team will choose one of these times to play:

	Sun July 28 at 3:00pm Eastern
	Wed July 31 at 9:00pm Eastern

Of course, the game can be rescheduled for a different time
if both captains agree.

The higher ranked team will be the home team.
